Video: You get to see @eliaselhardt’s last film Contraddiction? Well he just put out his new film Narcis! [Link in Bio] It is a beautiful portrait of a resort, and slice of mountain culture, frozen in time in Kosovo, framed by snowboarding and original film making. You’ll enjoy this short film, which is also now a staff pick at Vimeo! Better you here it from Elias: . “In times of rising nationalism and dispute, what brings us together!? With these questions in mind we traveled to Kosovo in eastern Europe and discovered the little ski town Brezovica. Once the place to be for winter sport in Yugoslavia, it fell apart during the Kosovo war just over 20 years ago. . Now snowboard enthusiast Hamdi is one of the locals that wants to breathe new life into this special place. Together with his friends he welcomed us with open arms and guided us through this forgotten world. After living there for a couple weeks we started to feel like home and in love with this little place where ethnical conflicts now give way to the shared passion for winter sports and the diversity of it’s community.”
